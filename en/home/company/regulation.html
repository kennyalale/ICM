    IC Markets is authorised and regulated by multiple Regulators across the globe.

    Trade with peace of mind knowing that IC Markets is supervised by some of the strictest regulatory bodies in the world.

    ASIC ASIC is the financial services regulator in Australia responsible for ensuring that financial markets in Australia are fair and transparent, supported by confident and informed investors and consumers. ASIC is an independent Commonwealth Government body established under the Australian Securities and Investments Commission Act (ASIC Act), carrying out most of its work under the Corporations Act.

    Australian Securities and
    Investments Commission

    IC Markets AU complies with ASIC regulatory requirements and has in place procedures to ensure that capital adequacy requirements are always maintained along with internal risk management and staff training requirements.

    External audits supplement IC Markets AU operational and accounting processes and ensure regulatory compliance.

    CySEC CySEC is an established regulatory authority governing financial services firms in Cyprus. Being a part of the European Union, CySEC is also a member of the European Securities and Markets Authority (ESMA). As a CySEC licensed firm, IC Markets EU complies with all domestic and European laws and regulations applicable to investment firms.

    Cyprus Securities and
    Exchange Commission

    IC Markets EU complies with CySEC regulatory requirements and is required to meet strict financial standards, including capital adequacy and audit requirements.

    IC Markets EU is committed to having strict management discipline and first-class controls in our compliance environment.


    FSA FSA is the financial services regulator in Seychelles responsible for ensuring that financial markets in Seychelles are fair and transparent, supported by confident and informed investors and consumers. The Seychelles Financial Services Authority is the autonomous regulatory body responsible for the non-bank financial services in the Seychelles. Established under the Financial Services Authority Act, 2013.

    Financial Services Authority

    IC Market Global complies with the FSA regulatory requirements and has in place internal risk management controls to ensure that it is sufficiently capitalized to support its operations.

    External audits supplement IC Markets Global operational and accounting process and ensure full regulatory compliance.
    SCB SCB is the Securities Commission of The Bahamas responsible for regulating and overseeing investment funds, securities and the capital markets. The Securities Commission of The Bahamas’s mission is to effectively oversee and regulate the activities of securities and the capital markets, to protect investors while strengthening public and institutional confidence in the integrity of those markets.

    Securities Commission Bahamas

    IC Markets Ltd. trading as IC Markets International complies with SCB regulatory requirements and has in place internal risk management controls to ensure sufficient capital requirements are always maintained.

    External audits supplement IC Markets International operational and accounting process and ensure full regulatory compliance.
